# Iteration 0001 Core Plan

## Problem Framing

The Core Design Team should define the specific OpenClinXR MVP planning problem in one page or less.

## Proposed MVP Approach

Describe the first wedge, first users, first institution-facing promise, and what is deliberately excluded.

## Architecture

Define the minimum architecture needed for an MVP planning packet: frontend, XR runtime, backend, trace store, analytics, faculty workflow, and deployment posture.

## Clinical And Educational Model

Define target learners, faculty roles, scenario authoring/review, debriefing, remediation, and specialty constraints.

## Psychometric Model

Define formative score use, evidence gates, rater workflow, fairness checks, trace completeness, and claims that must remain out of scope.

## Data And Trace Model

Define the first trace events, replay needs, audit exports, scenario versioning, and research export boundaries.

## Security, Privacy, Legal, And Compliance Model

Define consent, role access, learner records, retention, third-party tools, IP provenance, procurement controls, and counsel-ready questions.

## UX And Workflow Model

Define learner, faculty, admin, reviewer, and researcher workflows with attention to cognitive load and institutional adoption.

## Business And GTM Model

Define design partners, paid pilot packaging, pricing assumptions, proof points, renewal logic, and market risks.

## Open Risks

List every critical, high, and medium risk with owner and mitigation.

## Next Decisions

List decisions required before Senior Leadership can approve the plan.
