# Rubric

Every Core Plan and Adversarial Counterplan is scored from 0 to 5.

## Score Scale

- **0**: Missing or dangerous.
- **1**: Present but weak, vague, or misleading.
- **2**: Partially useful but materially incomplete.
- **3**: Adequate for early planning.
- **4**: Strong and actionable.
- **5**: Excellent, defensible, and ready for the next maturity gate.

Weights total 100.

## Improvement Rule

An iteration is improving when:

- Composite score rises, or stays stable while critical risk count decreases.
- No high-weight dimension regresses by more than 0.3 without explanation.
- Evidence debt decreases or is explicitly reclassified.
- Decision debt decreases or is explicitly deferred.
- Adversarial robustness increases after challenge cycles.

## Required Scorecard Fields

Each scorecard must include:

- Iteration ID.
- Plan type.
- Scored by.
- Dimension scores.
- Critical risks.
- Evidence debt.
- Decision debt.
- Confidence.
- Summary.
